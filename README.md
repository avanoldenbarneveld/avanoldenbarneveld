# Hey there! 

My name is Alberto.

I’m a software developer, and I define myself as someone who mostly thinks in systems. I have a tendency to always look at the bigger picture and try be conscious of how what I do fits into everything that I see. I believe that code is a means to an end, and every implementation of it a tradeoff. The end is making a product that solves very specific problems, which is what most time should be spent thinking about.

In my journey as a software developer, I have mainly three goals:

1. To be the best software developer I can be.
2. To belong to a team that makes the individuals in it better by the virtue of being in it.
3. To make a great product that genuinely helps people.

I currently work at Inbiot, where I build and maintain the MyInbiot platform, a cloud-based product that collects and visualizes indoor air quality data.

My work covers React and React Native on the front end, and Node.js, Python, and AWS on the back end.


Outside of code, I enjoy weightlifting, sim racing, and brewing coffee, all endlessly deep hobbies that become more rewarding the more time you invest in them.

I have a website where I try to make sense of what I see and feel by writing out what I think. You can visit it [here](https://albertovan.com).

If you have questions or are just curious don't doubt to contact me. Seriously. I'm always happy to chat.
